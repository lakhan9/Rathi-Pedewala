Our Heritage

Serving Akola since 1981 — one family’s promise of purity.

In 1981 we opened our doors with a simple vow: milk, ghee, and patience above all. The Rathi name became synonymous with the peda — and with the trust of neighbours who returned every festival for the same slow-cooked sweetness.

More than four decades on, we still batch carefully, source honestly, and let mithai rest until aroma and grain feel right — because legacy is not a slogan on a box; it is what you taste on the tongue.
